Help - BlueColar Form Builder

How to Use the Form Builder

  1. Click on the "Forms" button in the sidebar, then select "Form Builder" to start creating a new form.
  2. Use the field types in the left panel to add fields to your form. You can either click on a field type or drag and drop it into the form preview area.
  3. Click on a field in the form preview to edit its properties in the right panel.
  4. Use the "Preview" button to see how your form will look to users.
  5. Click "Save Form" to save your form. You'll be prompted to enter a name for the form.
  6. To load a previously saved form, click on "Load Form" and select the form from the dropdown list.
  7. To delete a form, select it from the dropdown list and click "Delete Form".

Additional Features

  • Custom Fields: Create and use custom field types for your forms.
  • Templates: Save and load form templates for quick form creation.
  • Settings: Customize the form builder and manage your account settings.

For more detailed instructions, please refer to our user manual.
