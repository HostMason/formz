How to use the Form Builder

  1. Drag and drop field types from the left panel to the form preview area.
  2. Click on a field in the preview area to edit its properties in the right panel.
  3. Use the buttons at the top to preview, save, load, or delete forms.
  4. To create a new form, simply start adding fields to an empty form preview area.
